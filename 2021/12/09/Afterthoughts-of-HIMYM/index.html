Afterthoughts of HIMYM

So I’ve been watching HIMYM for all two days. And I’ve watched 58 episodes so far. This is not good especially when it is just before the final week.

I really love this show. Actually I get obsessed with it.

At first, it was because of Robin. I mean how can someone not love Robin? She’s stunning, smart, friendly, beautiful, independent and tolerant. Not to mention she’s got an special American(or Canadian) sense of humour, which attracted me even more.

QUOTE

首先，她很优秀，nice face .nice job.然后，她对于身边的人理解并包容，如果说很多时候lily都在为ted和barney出谋划策，而robin就是那个会静静听你说完话的人，而且不会judge,有这么一个女生可以和她分享各种你做的坏事但不会被她道德批判其实挺难得的。同时她是一个外冷内热没有安全感的人，这种和她父亲从小对她的高要求有关，像个男孩子不喜欢轻易的表达自己，对待身边的人她的态度看似冷漠，却会默默给你关心和支持，很多时候robin希望被关心，喜欢被关心，但从未习惯被关心，所以当ted总是体察robin的情绪，给她各种各样无可救药的浪漫主义者的惊喜，robin是感动的，但是难以回报的，这种过于认真的感情，她感到压力而且不能习惯，barney才是那个能让她放心去爱的人，因为他们是一类人，才是真正的吸引，不是带有迁就和歉疚的感情。
https://www.zhihu.com/question/26397893/answer/185365587

And then, Ted. When Ted was dancing on the roof to make it rain so that Robin can’t go on the trip with her co-worker, I’m so touched. And when he said “I don’t want perfect, I want Robin” and “Victoria is great, but come on, that’s Robin”, I TOTALLY GET THE FEELING.

Guess I have been Ted once. There’s always a Robin. And I know the screenwriter of HIMYM must have a Robin himself because he LITERALLY graduated from wesleyan university where exactly Ted graduated from. We both know the feeling, it’s not excellent. But this kind of experience kinda shapes you who you are and the stunning girl will always be in your memory and say hi to you.

我一直相信，Robin这个人物是有其原型的，胖编剧的心里一定有这样一个爱而不得的姑娘。于是我一度以为编剧太爱Robin，所以急着在剧本里实现自己的愿望，但在看了无数遍《寻妈记》之后，我开始意识到，编剧的确想实现自己的愿望，但也仅仅是想实现愿望罢了，他或许并没有那么爱Robin。或者说，他并没有那么爱Robin的原型那个姑娘。
