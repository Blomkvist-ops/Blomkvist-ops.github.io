Detectives, Dream, and Life

I made a lot of usernames from detectives.

BKBK is for Blomkvist, a reporter from the film The Gril with the Dragon Tattoo. This name also stands for a young amateur detective called Kalle Blomkvist, who is a character from a series of children’s books. My username on WeChat is “Pineapple”, which has the similar pronounciation to Poirot, the famous detective written by Agatha Christie, in Chinese. ML is for Marlowe, the private detective from The Long Goodbye. I think the book is one of the best detective stories in the world. Holmes for… Guess no introduction needed here.

I always wanted to become a detective when I was young. Actually I’ve had a lot of dreams when I grow up. Most of them are about love, curiosity and the ultimate goal that I’m rich enough to quit any job one day. LOL

I don’t even know why I’m writing this. Guess I truly have nothing to write but still don’t want to do leetcode problems. Getting lazier. Have to start tomorrow as well as my driver’s license study.

Anyway, I found a cute poem on Instagram yesterday. Hope whoever reading this has a nice day. :)
